Welcome to Trove Mod Manager.

Dusty_Mustard and many others of the Trove population put out a request to the community to create a new mod loader program on the forums, and here is the result of responding to that request.

Features

  • Installs mods manually from a .zip file located on your local computer, removes mods, and clears out all mods from all overrides
  • Auto-detects Live folder for user-friendliness
  • Searches for mods found on the Trovesaurus database and installs them directly all from within the program
  • Includes Mod Creation Tools such as Archive Extraction to help fellow modders quickly update their files
